Understanding Haircomb
************************

Haircomb has a unique and complex transaction structure. While this makes for a very interesting cryptocurrency, it also means that it can be difficult to understand how it all works. The goal of this documentation to to help you learn about how Haircomb functions, so you can be confident during every step of a transaction.

Certain pages will be split up into a Basic and Advanced section; the Basic sections go over the general desciption of the current topic, whereas the Advanced sections get into the specific mechanics of how the topic works. If it's your first time reading, you may wish to skip the Advanced sections for now, and come back to them once you have a better understanding of the overall workings of Haircomb.
